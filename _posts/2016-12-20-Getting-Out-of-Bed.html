Dear Lord, So far I've done all right. I haven't gossipped, haven't lost my temper, haven't been greedy, grumpy, nasty, selfish, or overindulgent.

I'm really glad about that.

And in a few minutes, God, I'm going to get out of bed. From then on, I'm going to need a lot more help.
