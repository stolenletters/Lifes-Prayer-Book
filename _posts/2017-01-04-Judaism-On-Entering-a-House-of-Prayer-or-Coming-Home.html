--- layout: post title: Judaism On Entering a House of Prayer or Coming Home source: Jewish Tradition author: section: On Occasions subSection: order: type: Adoration, Petition, Declaration, version: 38 note: see: WIL: ---

God In the abundance of your kindness I come into your house. I love the places where your glory dwells. As for me, I will prostrate before you. I will worship. May the words of my mouth and the meditations of my heart be acceptable. In an abundance of love, please answer me in truth with salvation.
