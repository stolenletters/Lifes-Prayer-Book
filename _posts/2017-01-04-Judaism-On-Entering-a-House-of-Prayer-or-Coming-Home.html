--- layout: default title: Judaism On Entering a House of Prayer or Coming Home source: Jewish Tradition author: section: On Occasions category: Adoration, Petition, Declaration, version: 37b note: see: WIL: ---

God In the abundance of your kindness I come into your house. I love the places where your glory dwells. As for me, I will prostrate before you. I will worship. May my prayer to you be acceptable. In the abundance of your love, please answer me in the truth of your salvation.
