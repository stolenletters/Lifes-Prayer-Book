--- layout: post title: With God source: Prayer a History Philip Zaleski, Carol Zaleski author: Saint Patrick section: On Occasions type: Petition, Intercession, Declaration, Refinement, version: 37b note: see: WIL: I like the God in me, God in everyone who speaks of me. I like the ambiguity of who I am praying for and who gains. ---

God here with me, before and behind me, beneath and above me, on my right and left. God in me. God where I lie, God where I sit, God where I arise, God in the heart of everyone who thinks of me, God in the mouth of every one who speaks to me, God in every eye that sees me, God in every ear that hears me. May your salvation be ever with us.
