F-Quakers Saying attributed to George Fox http://postmodernquaker.wordpress.com/2011/07/20/answering-that-of-god-part-1/ G-First Thessalonians 5:17 author: section: subSection: order: type: Petition, Refinement, version: 37b note: see: WIL: ---

I want to rejoice in the way (Torah), pray without ceasing, and give thanks in all circumstances.G Open my hart to the way (Torah), let me pursue our commandments,E let me do kindness, love mercy, and walk humbly.D I want to walk cheerfully over the world answering that of god in everyone.F It is time to give up my hopes for acceptance, my fears of rejection, and love generously. Please give me a resigned hart to love generously.
