--- layout: post title: On Going To The Bathroom source: Jewish Tradition author: section: On Occasions subSection: Common Occasions order: type: Adoration, Thanksgiving version: 37b note: see: WIL: ---

Blessed are you who formed me with wisdom and created within me many openings and many organs. If certain of them were open that should be blocked or blocked that should be open it would be impossible to stand before You. Blessed are you, who crates all flesh to act wondrously.
